## 4. Risk Analysis

### 4.1 Risk Rating Criteria

#### Likelihood Scale

| Score | Level          | Definition                 |
| ----- | -------------- | -------------------------- |
| 1     | Rare           | < 1% chance in 12 months   |
| 2     | Unlikely       | 1-25% chance in 12 months  |
| 3     | Possible       | 25-50% chance in 12 months |
| 4     | Likely         | 50-75% chance in 12 months |
| 5     | Almost Certain | > 75% chance in 12 months  |

#### Impact Scale

| Score | Level        | Financial   | Operational | Reputational  | Regulatory |
| ----- | ------------ | ----------- | ----------- | ------------- | ---------- |
| 1     | Negligible   | < $10K      | Minimal     | None          | None       |
| 2     | Minor        | $10K-$100K  | Limited     | Local         | Minor      |
| 3     | Moderate     | $100K-$500K | Significant | Regional      | Moderate   |
| 4     | Major        | $500K-$1M   | Severe      | National      | Major      |
| 5     | Catastrophic | > $1M       | Critical    | International | Severe     |

#### Risk Matrix

|                        | Negligible (1) | Minor (2) | Moderate (3) | Major (4) | Catastrophic (5) |
| ---------------------- | -------------- | --------- | ------------ | --------- | ---------------- |
| **Almost Certain (5)** | Medium         | High      | High         | Critical  | Critical         |
| **Likely (4)**         | Low            | Medium    | High         | High      | Critical         |
| **Possible (3)**       | Low            | Medium    | Medium       | High      | High             |
| **Unlikely (2)**       | Low            | Low       | Medium       | Medium    | High             |
| **Rare (1)**           | Low            | Low       | Low          | Medium    | Medium           |
